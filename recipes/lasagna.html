Homemade Lasagna Recipe

A simple yet delicious meaty and cheesy lasagna recipe. Pair it with fresh salad and garlic bread, and you've got the perfect cozy dinner for you and family.

Ingredients:

  • 1-pound of ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • Fresh herbs for flavor, parsley and garlic
  • Seasonings: Basil, Oregano, salt, pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cottage and parmesan cheese
  • Eggs

Cooking Steps:

  1. 1.Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.

  2. 2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.

  3. 3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.

  4. 4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.

  5. 5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown. (50 minutes total)
